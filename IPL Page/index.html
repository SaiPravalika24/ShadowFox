The Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) is a professional cricket franchise based in Hyderabad, India, that competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2012, SRH has quickly established itself as a formidable team in the league, known for its strong bowling lineup and aggressive batting. The franchise is owned by Sun TV Network and plays its home matches at the Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium. SRH has won the IPL title once, in 2016, and continues to attract a passionate fan base, emphasizing teamwork and resilience. With a mix of experienced players and young talents, SRH aims to consistently perform at the highest level and bring glory to its supporters.
